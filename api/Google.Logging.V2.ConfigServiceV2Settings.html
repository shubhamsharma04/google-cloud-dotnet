RetrySettings for asynchronous and synchronous calls to

CreateSink(String, LogSink, CallSettings) and CreateSinkAsync(String, LogSink, CallSettings).

CreateSinkAsync(String, LogSink, CallSettings) RetrySettings are:

  • Initial retry delay: 100 milliseconds
